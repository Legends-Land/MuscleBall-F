Who We Are

The Next Generation of Team Sports

Muscleball is a high-octane, physically demanding sport that blends the best elements of volleyball, basketball, and handball. Our mission is to create a community of passionate athletes who value strength, strategy, and teamwork. We believe in pushing boundaries and creating a game that's as exhilarating to watch as it is to play.

Whether you're a seasoned veteran or new to the sport, our leagues and events are designed to challenge you, help you grow, and connect you with a vibrant community. Join us and be a part of the movement.

Rules and Regulations

General Objective

The objective of Muscleball is to score points by landing the ball on the opposing team's side of the court. Teams consist of five players, with a single designated "Muscleballer" on each team who has special privileges.

How It‚Äôs Played

  • Teams: 2v2, 3v3, or 4v4 (5v5 on social days)
  • Ball: 8 lb medicine ball (coed), 6 lb (women‚Äôs)
  • Court: Standard volleyball court & net
  • No player may hit the ball twice in a row.

Core Rules

  • Serve with two hands only.
  • Rally scoring: every serve = a point.
  • Two steps max before throwing.
  • Always throw with both hands ‚Äî no one-handed throws.
  • Dunking is not allowed, but dropping the ball over the net while airborne is legal.

From Hoover Ball to Muscle Ball

In 1928, President Herbert Hoover and Dr. Joel Boone invented Hoover Ball, a medicine ball sport played on the White House lawn. Muscle Ball takes this tradition forward ‚Äî with modern rules, stronger athletes, and a growing community

Match Format

  • Matches are best-of-three sets. The first two sets are played to 25 points, with a two-point advantage needed to win.
  • The third set, if needed, is played to 15 points, also requiring a two-point advantage.
  • Teams switch sides after each set.
